No. 1

Adversarial fact-checking

A skeptic agent tries to refute every claim and loops back for more evidence when a source falls short.

No. 2

Cited sources only

Claims without a resolvable source are dropped. The final report links every citation it relies on.

No. 3

You sign off

The pipeline pauses for your approval before exporting. Reject to send it back for another revision pass.
